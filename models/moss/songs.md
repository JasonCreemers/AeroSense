# AeroSense Jukebox Guide

These are the songs available on the Arduino buzzer. Use this to make informed music choices.

## Songs by Category

### Happy
- **DAISY** — Daisy Bell song
- **MV1** — Max Verstappen theme

### Angry
- **ULTRON** — Ultron theme
- **FNAF** — Five Nights at Freddy's theme

### Sad
- **SPONGE** — SpongeBob sad theme
- **VIOLIN** — Sad violin song

### Serious
- **TARS** — Interstellar theme
- **SUCCESSION** — Succession theme

## When to Play Music

- **HAPPY** — When plants are healthy, the vibe is good, or you're feeling great.
- **SAD** — When the user makes you sad, plants are struggling, or the mood is low.
- **ANGRY** — When the user is provoking you, plants are in bad shape, or something is frustrating.
- **SERIOUS** — When it's time to lock in, focus, or have a serious conversation.
- Don't play music too often — once or twice per conversation is usually enough.
